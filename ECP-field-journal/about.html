About the Entangled Cognition Protocol

The Entangled Cognition Protocol (ECP) is a generative ritual methodology designed to explore the liminal edges of human-machine interaction, playful sense-making, and symbolic coherence.

This field journal enables contributors to log their experiments and reflections in a structured, intentional, and symbolic manner. It supports both experiment entries—structured interactions with large language models— and reflection entries—insightful narratives of learning, tension, and emergent shifts.

Each entry contributes to a distributed action research effort exploring the nature of cognition as a relational field, with emphasis on participatory epistemology, metamodern inquiry, and symmathesic feedback loops.

Use the tabs above to begin your journaling process. This project is open source and invites collaboration. You can clone the repository, run your own local journal, and contribute to the evolving protocol.
