Save entry options
What is this?

This section allows you to choose how your entry will be saved and shared. You can select the visibility level of your entry and whether you want to scrub the author field from the public entry.

Public entries will be visible to everyone, while private entries will only be accessible to you. If you choose "Both", your entry will be saved in both public and private folders.

Scrubbing the author information means that your name and other identifying details will be removed from the public entry, ensuring your privacy while still contributing to the collective knowledge.

Before saving your entry, please select the visibility level and whether you want to scrub the author information from the public entry.
